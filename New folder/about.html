about

I am from St.Louis Mo and now live in Urbana Il. I was a herion addict for 14 years I made it out alive only by the grace of God. I have been making comedy skits on video since I was a kid and now I do all the videography for the church I attend and Lifeline connect residential treatment center. I went to visual and performing arts high school and forest park college in videography and fine arts. I play guitar and make beats, I try to sing but I am not very good at it. Oh well its still fun. I was in a couple punk bands as a kid and a metal band in my mid 20s. Im 35 years old but act alot younger.

work

I directed and did the camera work on Your love is killin me documentary about addiction. I have produced various songs for different rappers and helped produce "beyond" a live and video drama for Life student ministries.
